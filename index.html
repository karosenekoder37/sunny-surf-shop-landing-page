Our Story

Local

Est. in 2003, Sunny Surf Shop has been proudly servicing the locals of Lahaina, Kaanapali, and Napili for over 20 years now. We strive to give back to the community that has supported us. 10% of our profits go towards our foundation, Surfing Education, that supports native Maui students pay for college education.

Family Owned & Operated

Sunny Surf Shop was founded by Leilani and Kai. They both are natives to Maui, born and raised.Both of them love surfing and always dreamed of opening up their own shop to share that love. Their parents, Kao and Nani, also help out around the shop & their daughter, Alana, enjoys greeting all the customers!

Guarantee

Our promise to you is that you will absolutely love our products guaranteed or your money back. We truly stand behind not only our work, but also our customers. We want to make sure you are 100% content with your new surfboard. If within the first 30 days of your purchase you aren't 100% satisfied, you get 100% of your money back. After those 30 days, you have an additional 30 days to get 100% store credit to put towards another surfboard. Additionally, for only $30 annaully, you get free maintenance checkups and repairs for minor damages including dings, small cracks, delamnation, loose screws, and small chips in the paint. You are family, ohana, and family stand by each other. So, we stand by you and want to make sure our products can stand by you throughout the years.
